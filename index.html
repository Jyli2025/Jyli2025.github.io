Biography

I am a final-year undergraduate student majoring in New Energy Materials and Devices in the School of New Energy, North China Electric Power University (NCEPU), with a GPA ranking of 2/28. I have been admitted to the Institute of Chemistry, Chinese Academy of Sciences (ICCAS) for a combined Master-Ph.D. program, starting in September 2026.

I will join the research group of Academician Yongfang Li, under the supervision of Prof. Lei Meng, whose mentorship lineage traces back to Prof. Yang Yang from UCLA.

Future Graduate Research:

Research Experience

  • Served as the Principal Investigator for a provincial-level "College Student Innovation and Entrepreneurship Training Program" project: Structural Design of Chiral Metamaterials and the Mechanism of Optical Activity Modulation.
  • Contributed to the "Three Gorges Group Cooperation Project" and the State Grid Suzhou Power Supply Bureau project: Research on Key Technologies for Ultra-Short-Term Distributed Photovoltaic Power Generation Forecasting.

  • Contributed to three manuscripts currently under review.
